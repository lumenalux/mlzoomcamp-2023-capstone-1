About the Credit Evaluation Project

This project utilizes XGBoost, Random Forest and Linear Regression models to predict the likelihood of individuals facing difficulties in repaying credit. It's designed to assist financial institutions in making informed decisions. Users can input various personal and financial details to receive an instant prediction.

Input Field Descriptions

  • CNT_CHILDREN: The number of children the individual has.
  • AMT_INCOME_TOTAL: The total income of the individual.
  • AMT_CREDIT: The total credit amount requested by the individual.
  • AMT_ANNUITY: The annuity amount of the loan.
  • AMT_GOODS_PRICE: The price of the goods for which the loan is given.
  • REGION_POPULATION_RELATIVE: The relative population of the region where the individual lives.
  • DAYS_BIRTH: The age of the individual in days (negative value).
  • DAYS_EMPLOYED: The number of days the individual has been employed (negative value).
  • DAYS_REGISTRATION: The number of days since the individual's registration, relative to the current application.
  • DAYS_ID_PUBLISH: The number of days since the individual's identification was published.
  • FLAG_WORK_PHONE: A binary flag indicating whether the individual has a work phone (1 for yes, 0 for no).
  • REGION_RATING_CLIENT: The rating of the region where the individual resides, on a scale of 1 to 3.
  • HOUR_APPR_PROCESS_START: The hour of the day when the loan application process started.
